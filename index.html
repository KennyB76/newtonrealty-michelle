About Michelle

Quiet, deliberate, deeply personal.

Michelle has spent decades doing one thing: making sure you leave looking and feeling like yourself — only better. Trained and refined at KAKABOKA, Manhattan's Koreatown landmark with nearly 30 years of heritage.

She has built a loyal following among clients with discerning taste — and, often, the kind of thick, stubborn Asian hair that demands real expertise. She doesn't rush. She listens. And when she's done, you'll notice the difference — not in a loud way, but in the way your hair moves.
